Class IV Laser Therapy

Class IV Laser therapy is an effective medical treatment for acute and chronic pains. Some of the many advantages of laser therapy included decreasing pain, reducing muscle spasms, and improving circulation to injured tissue. This treatment is a non-invasive, non-pharmacological alternative for pain relief.

How Does Laser Therapy Work?

The wavelength of the laser used in Class IV Laser therapy penetrates deeply into the nerve, muscle, ligament, and tendon tissue in a short amount of time. Class IV Laser stimulates the production of ATP, an energy molecule within the body cells. This increased production of the energy source helps speed up the healing process. We use Class IV Laser therapy on a range of issues from acute injuries to chronic conditions such as herniated discs or degeneration. It is a safe and effective methods to provide relief for our patients.
