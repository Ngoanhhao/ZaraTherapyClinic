Foot Orthotics

1. What Is an Orthotic?

Orthotics are a special shoe inserts that can be used to treat numerous foot conditions. There are many different types of orthotics, and they will vary in materials and features based on you specific conditions.

2. How Do Orthotics Work?

A custom orthotic is a prescribed medical device from a doctor. Custom means, a doctor uses casts, impressions, or scans to perfectly fit your foot. A doctor-designed orthotic will control the alignment and function of your foot. These orthotics will treat and prevent abnormal motion or rolling of the foot.
